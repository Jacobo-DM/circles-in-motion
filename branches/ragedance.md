# RageDance (Branch)

RageDance is a lineage-branch within **Circles in Motion**.

It represents the originating “fire” thread: a way of transforming emotional intensity into circular flow without domination, coercion, or collapse. The emphasis is not aggression; it is **return** — energy moving through the body and back into center.

## Relationship to Circles in Motion

- **Circles in Motion** is the root public-facing name and framework.
- **RageDance** is a specific branch with its own history, language, and development path.

## What this branch protects

- The right to feel without acting out harm  
- Circular motion as a regulator (not a performance)  
- Strength without hardening  
- Flow that returns to the body

## Notes on language

This branch is being refined for clarity and cultural neutrality where needed, while preserving the original intent and integrity. Redundancies will be cleaned over time without erasing the source lineage.
