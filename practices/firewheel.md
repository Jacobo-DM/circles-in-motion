# FireWheel

FireWheel is a bilateral spiral movement practice used in RageDance and Circles in Motion.

## Entry

Shake out the hands as if drying them quickly.

Allow the arms to extend while the body begins rocking and swaying.

Rhythm first. Structure later.

---

## Interlaced Negotiation

Interlace the fingers.

Rotate the wrists in circles and reverse direction.

One side will tend to dominate.

Notice it. Flip the leadership.

The goal is not symmetry.

Both sides learn to move together.

---

## Open Palm Expansion

When the body feels coordinated and safe, the hands may separate naturally.

Palms face each other and move in circular loops through space.

The movement may spread through the body:

hands → arms → shoulders → hips → spine → legs.

The body becomes a dance of circles.

---

## Self-Regulation Loop

sensation → tune-up → movement → resonance

Tune-ups may include:

- prod  
- squeeze  
- shake  
- wiggle  

If the area wakes up → go time.  
If not → rest time.

---

## Closing

The movement may slow into stillness, walking, or free dance.

Before finishing:

Sit or kneel.

Breathe.

Hydrate.

Allow the body to return to center.
